About Us

STEM MAL is the team name of our organization, which stands for STEM High School for Boys-6th of October, and MAL stands for Model Arab League. Our team was the first to be established in the STEM community about four years ago by a junior student named Farid Wahba. He initiated this idea as a student-led organization aimed at helping fellow students enhance certain skills that are often lacking at the beginning of their STEM journey. These skills include public speaking, communication, and critical thinking, all approached from a political perspective. Farid identified key areas for improvement, allowing us to pursue careers and interests beyond STEM subjects. Consequently, we created a platform for a political conference where each delegate represents a country and engages in discussions with other delegates regarding specific issues in the Arab World.
