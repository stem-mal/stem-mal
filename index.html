About

About MAL

STEM MAL, from STEM High School for Boys-6th of October, merges Model Arab League (MAL) activities. Founded by Farid Wahba four years ago, it hones STEM students' skills through political engagement, fostering critical thinking and communication.

Our conference platform fosters discussions on pressing Arab World issues, cultivating vital skills and global awareness, preparing individuals for diverse challenges.
