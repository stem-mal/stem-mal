Conference

Conference refers to a formal meeting or gathering of delegates who come together to discuss a specific topic or issue. Conferences are typically organized with the purpose of sharing information, exchanging ideas, and fostering collaboration among participants and talking specifically about some of the issues that the Arab countries face in a formal way and gaining new skills like public speaking and diplomacy.
